Business Ventures

"I didn’t want to look like a consultant — I wanted to look like a corporation."

🧠 iConcepts (1978)

Origin: Founded over a weekend after Hayden left his job and clients called him directly. Rented an office, bought a desk, and hired a receptionist — all before Monday.

Services: Custom CRM platforms, salesforce automation, and client analytics software. Built to help businesses understand customer behavior and predict needs.

Impact: One of the first small businesses to implement predictive analytics and CRM personalization before it was a norm. Helped clients increase reorder rates by up to 60%.

"You have to look like somebody before they treat you like somebody."

💼 BOS Software

Origin: Born from conversations with AB Dick distributors who were using outdated systems. Hayden saw a gap and acted.

Services: Enterprise-level business operations software (ERP). Provided accounting, inventory, and sales processing in one ecosystem — adapted from international platforms, localized for U.S. businesses.

Strategy: Hayden didn’t start from scratch. He reverse-engineered proven systems, restructured them for niche U.S. markets, and delivered clean, affordable software.

"Why build something new when you can optimize what already works?"

🏢 Southwestern Underwriters

Origin: Hayden recognized that many of his software clients lacked proper risk coverage and insurance guidance.

Services: Specialized insurance and underwriting services for small-to-mid sized tech-forward businesses. Focused on offering business continuity, data security, and liability coverage.

Integration: Combined software insights with insurance risk modeling — a rare hybrid approach. Provided underwriting services directly aligned with client data.

"Every business I built came from a gap the last one uncovered."

🔄 Cross-Business Strategy

Hayden’s ventures weren’t silos — they were parts of a larger system. Software predicted client needs. Insurance protected those needs. All services were based on trust, efficiency, and simplicity.

By connecting businesses horizontally, Hayden retained clients for decades — offering what most couldn't: seamless continuity, strong ethics, and long-term reliability.

"Three companies. One vision: make systems smarter, safer, and more human."
